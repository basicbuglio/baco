About

Experience and quality

Our company started manufacturing springs back in 1986. What was once a small family business, today is a reputable company in spring manufacturing field that supplies whole region with it's products.

Our product line has a wide array of springs and spring plated products. We can manufacture springs from Ø 0,30mm up to Ø 17,00mm. We are able to manufacture springs using our own templates or the ones you provide for us.

Ever since the beginning we have been following industry trends in the industry and technology. Our products are made from highest quality materials and we have ISO standards needed in today's day and age.

owner Svetislav Pajic Mechanical Engineer
