SZR Baco Springs without compromise in quality and price

SZR Baco - Cacak

Back in 1986, we founded our company with clearly defined principles - high manufacturing quality and affordable prices. Today, more than 30 years later, we still adhere to the same principles in manufacturing springs for you.

We are able to offer different kinds of springs with thickness ranging from Ø 0,30mm up to Ø17mm as well as plated springs products. We can manufacture springs with non-standard dimensions made by your specifications.

We are keeping up with all contemporary trends in the industry and we have all the standards needed. Our products come with all the necessary certificates.
